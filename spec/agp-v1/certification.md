# AGP v1 Certification Contract Specification

## Overview
Defines the trust signaling surface of the protocol. Ensures ecosystem participants understand what guarantees are offered by AGP badges.

## Specification Areas
1. **Badge Semantics**: Meaning of cryptographic classifications.
2. **Certification Envelope Structure**: Schema for the badge payload.
3. **Dataset Certification Guarantees**: Asserting structural accuracy.
4. **Policy Portability Guarantees**: Ensuring runs anywhere.
5. **Migration Safety Guarantees**: Reversibility checks.
6. **Maturity Classification Guarantees**: Enterprise readiness scoring.
7. **Adapter Certification Guarantees**: Conformance to execution models.
