# Governance Observability Contract

The Arch-Engine Governance Observability Fabric enables proactive detection and forecasting of topology instability and regression velocity across the ecosystem.

## Declarations

Arch-Engine supports:
1. **Predictive Governance Observability**: Aggregating raw signals from datasets, capability matrices, and topology states.
2. **Topology Drift Early-Warning Detection**: Predicting when capabilities are accelerating away from the desired policy baseline.
3. **Ecosystem Instability Radar**: Detecting clusters of regression propagation across multi-tenant environments.
4. **Capability Regression Forecasting**: Analyzing adoption collapse momentum to prevent widespread architectural regressions.
5. **Dataset Schema Fragility Forecasting**: Predicting structural breakages before migration campaigns are executed.
