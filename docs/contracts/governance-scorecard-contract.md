# Executive Intelligence Contract

The Arch-Engine Governance Scorecard Runtime provides executive-grade intelligence, attributing governance operations directly to ROI and industry percentile metrics.

## Declarations

Arch-Engine supports:
1. **Executive Architecture Scorecards**: Generating deterministic stability, resilience, and safety scores.
2. **Policy ROI Attribution Measurement**: Tracing specific policy implementations to quantifiable capability impact.
3. **Capability Adoption Effectiveness Scoring**: Measuring the regression avoidance impact of strict capability enforcement.
4. **Dataset Resilience Improvement Scoring**: Attributing specific migration events to improvements in dataset resilience.
5. **Migration Success Attribution Scoring**: Quantifying the organizational impact of `MigrationCampaignRuntime` executions.
6. **Industry Maturity Percentile Benchmarking**: Correlating internal resilience scores with federated industry adoption metrics.
