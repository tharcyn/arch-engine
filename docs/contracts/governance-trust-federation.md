# Governance Trust Federation Contract

The Arch-Engine Governance Trust Federation enables a globally verifiable ecosystem where multiple distinct certification authorities can interoperate safely.

## Declarations

Arch-Engine supports:
1. **Distributed Certification Authority Federation**: Decoupling certification trust from a single organizational boundary.
2. **Multi-Issuer Trust Chain Validation**: Following certification signatures back to recognized global anchors.
3. **Independent Verification Nodes**: Allowing third-party platforms to verify badge authenticity without full engine ingestion.
4. **Certification Transparency Lineage Tracking**: Immutable ledgering of certification creation and expiration.
5. **Delegated Authority Trust Propagation**: Permitting parent authorities to grant specific certification rights to trusted sub-organizations.
6. **Revocation Visibility Propagation**: Synchronously broadcasting certificate revocations across the global trust network.
